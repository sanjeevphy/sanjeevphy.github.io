Submission Guidelines

  • Word limit: 250–400 words.
  • The abstract should clearly state the purpose, key results, and major conclusions of the research.
  • Submissions must be original and plagiarism-free (similarity index below 10%).
  • Authors are required to select the appropriate track during submission.
  • Accepted abstracts will be assigned an Abstract ID and reviewed by the Programme Committee.
  • The abstract, paper, and poster templates will be updated soon, and submissions will open shortly.
  • Submission of a full paper is not mandatory to present a paper or attend ICAPST.

Journal Publication

  • Authors can submit full manuscript in conference-aligned journals after conference.
  • The acceptance of paper solely depends on review process and academic merit of the delegates' submissions.
  • We will make every effort to ensure that accepted papers are free of any article processing charges (APCs).
