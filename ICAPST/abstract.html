Submission Guidelines

Word limit: 250–400 words.

It should briefly state the purpose, the principal results, and the major conclusions of the research.

Must be original and plagiarism-free (<10%).

Select appropriate Tracks during submission.

Accepted abstracts will receive an Abstract ID and will be reviewed by the programme committee.

Abstract, Paper & Poster template will be updated soon.

Full paper submission is not required in order to present paper or attend ICAPST.
