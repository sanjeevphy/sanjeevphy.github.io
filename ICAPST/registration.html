Guidelines

  • The registration fee covers a conference kit, access to domain-specific sessions, a presentation/participation certificate, and food coupons during the conference.
  • A paper presentation certificate will be issued to the presenter. The other co-authors will be issued participation certificates if registered individually.
  • Certificates will be issued to the Attendees.
  • Concession available on group registration.
  • The ICAPST abstract book will be made available online in digital formats.

Online Mode

ICAPST encourages all presenters and attendees to participate in person. However, an online session will be available for those facing unforeseen travel or health issues.

No fee concessions will be provided for this option. Participation in the online session will be granted upon request, and only a limited number of presentations will be accommodated.
